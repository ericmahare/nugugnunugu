Join us for one of the most beautiful celebrations of wildlife and extraordinary bush experience with a combination of extensive mobile safari game drives in an open 4×4 game drive vehicle in Moremi Game Reserve, and guided Mokoro (traditional dugout canoe) safaris on the untouched waterways of the Okavango Delta and an optional scenic helicopter flight. We will travel between various locations based around the larger Okavango Delta for a comprehensive wildlife and nature experience.

DAY 1: Arrival and Sunset Mokoro Safari
You will be picked up at the airport where you will meet your ranger and guide. Proceed on the 20-minute drive to your tented lodge situated on the banks of the Boteti River. Upon arrival at the lodge, you will check in.

Depending on your time of arrival, you will have some time for a quick lunch, a dip in the pool, or to explore the lodge’s natural surroundings before you leave on an amazing 2-hour sunset mokoro safari on the Boteti River. Your experienced mokoro guide will point out several interesting aspects of the river and its wildlife during your mokoro trip. You will stop on one of the banks for a small refreshment while enjoying the magnificent sunset.

DAY 2: Okavango Delta Mokoro Safari and Bush Walk
Waking up at sunrise with the first birdcalls of the day, you will enjoy the crisp morning air before having some freshly brewed coffee/tea and some home baked biscuits prepared for you. A packed breakfast will be supplied to you to enjoy on your way to the boating station at the Okavango Delta.

Your Mokoro safari will begin as you head out into the many reed channels of the Okavango Delta. Your guide will already have an update of any exciting or special sightings in the area.

DAY 3: Mobile Camping Safari into Moremi Game Reserve area
After breakfast, you can get your things ready for your mobile tented safari. You will be met and briefed by your guide at the reception area. You will cover a large distance over the next few days with extensive game drives most of the days.

Today, you will also pass through the Khwai area. Khwai is a wildlife-rich 200,000 hectare area sandwiched between the Moremi Game Reserve and Chobe National Park.

DAY 4: Game Drive & return to Boteti River camp
Rising at the crack of dawn, you will wake up to the sounds of birdcalls near and far. As you step out of your tent you will be greeted by friendly faces and a freshly brewed cup of tea or coffee. A delicious, cooked breakfast will be prepared to provide energy for the day ahead. Final game drive, returning to our wonderful base camp positioned right beside the stunning Boteti River.

DAY 5: Optional Scenic Helicopter Flight over Okavango Delta & Departure
This morning, a delicious breakfast and freshly brewed coffee/tea will be served. You will have most of the morning at leisure to enjoy the peaceful atmosphere at camp.

Journey with us to the other worldly landscapes of Africa and discover astonishing earth most secluded safari destinations.
